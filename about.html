ABOUT

Textile designer Andrea Óvári has a passion for design – this passion comes to life in textile items of all varieties.

Andrea's bespoke soft furnishings are designed with their function in mind, and stylized with a light-hearted and playful sense of decor. The unique curtains, carpets and cushions boast well-thought-out patterns, and refined harmonies of colour. The items are designed to blend into the interior, yet still bear the characteristics of their setting.

When working on assignments, Andrea pays special attention to the client's requirements – all design work is carried out with the goals and constraints in mind. A typical assignment begins with a requirements analysis and site inspection, followed by measurement, design, and finally, installation of the finished soft furnishings, curtains and poles.
